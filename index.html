Step 2 — Go to Topview (or any site)

Open topview.ai in Safari normally. Log in as usual.

Step 3 — Tap the bookmarklet

Tap the address bar, type "Mobile" (or whatever you named it), and tap it.

A floating panel appears on screen with zoom controls.

Tap it again to remove the panel. All fixes are temporary — refresh to reset.

What it does

  • Fixes viewport so pinch-zoom works
  • Auto-scales page to fit your screen width
  • Makes buttons & inputs finger-sized (44px)
  • Floating + / − zoom controls
  • Hide/show fixed headers & footers
  • Works on ANY website you're already logged into
  • No app, no install, no server needed
